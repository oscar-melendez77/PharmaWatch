§00Abstract

PharmaWatch compiles the scattered evidence on a medication's real-world risk — the FDA's adverse-event reports, the peer-reviewed literature, the official label, and what patients actually say — into a single per-drug safety record. Four gradient-boosted models score serious reactions, hospitalization, death and disability; a transparent pass adjusts them to the reader's own profile; and a grounded agent answers the follow-up questions. Every figure below is produced by one reproducible pipeline.
