§00Abstract

PharmaWatch compiles the scattered evidence on a medication's real-world risk — the FDA's adverse-event reports, the peer-reviewed literature, the official label, and what patients actually say — into a single per-drug safety record. Four gradient-boosted models score serious reactions, hospitalization, death and disability; a grounded agent then re-weights them for the reader's profile — age, sex, alcohol, smoking — before answering follow-up questions. Every figure is produced by one reproducible pipeline; the public demo below runs on illustrative sample data.
